About the Grand Croatian Dictionary

I created the dictionary out of the frustration of trying to learn this language without many resources. The concept of declension was not easy to grasp and I found it difficult to expand my vocabulary, since Croatian words can take many forms and there are many irregular words. Building the dictionary has helped me learn the language faster.

The database was built mainly by scrapping Wiktionary articles from en.wiktionary.org and sh.wiktionary.org. I really appreciate the effort put into this open-source dictionaries. What I simply did was gather the information from these sites, organized it in a structured database and put it in an Android app for easier access.

Using the Grand Croatian Dictionary I was also able to gain some insight on the most frequently used words. This was useful to speed up the learning process.
